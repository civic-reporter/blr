üîí Data Privacy

We Do NOT Store Your Data

  • No user data collection: We do not collect, store, or retain any personal information, user accounts, or identifiable data.
  • No image storage: Photos you upload are processed in your browser and posted directly to X. We do not store images on our servers.
  • No location tracking: GPS coordinates are extracted from your photos or selected on the map, used only for the report, and not stored by us.
  • AI processing: Face detection and blurring (for traffic reports) happens in your browser using client-side AI before transmission. After face blurring, images are sent to AWS Rekognition for content moderation to prevent misuse. Text descriptions are analyzed using AWS Comprehend to prevent abusive language. These AWS services process data transiently for moderation purposes only and do not retain your content.
